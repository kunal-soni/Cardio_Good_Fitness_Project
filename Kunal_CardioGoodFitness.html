Conclusion and Recommendations¶

1) Product - It looks like TM798 is sold more to higher income people, and there are very few women that have bought it. This can be advertised better to women. Also, as the people that use this product do a lot more miles, it could be worth checking to see if price for this can be reduced a bit, so that more people can buy this product and have better miles and fitness, as those are positively correlated.

2) From the correlation heatmap of six numerical variables, it shows that Fitness-Miles, Miles-Usage have high positive correlation > 0.75. This can be positively represented to general population, that people who use the Cardio Good Fitness treadmills more are more fit. Fitness-Usage also has a positive correlation 0.67. Usage-Income also has positive correlation of 0.52. However there could be a scope of reducing price, or creating a fourth treadmill product to cater to lower-income people.

3) Gender - From the countplot of gender, we see single females are the lowest buyers. The product can be advertised better to them, or made in different colors, to be attractive for them to buy.

4) Age - From the countplot of age, we see that more users for this treadmill are between age 23-28. There are some users in 30's but a lot less in 40's. There could be some sales targeted to show how people above 40 could also use this product and be more fit.
